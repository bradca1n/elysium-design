How our pricing works

POD · Confidential

You only pay POD as you earn. POD charges no upfront fees, no fixed fees and no minimum fees. We win when you win.

POD charges a percentage of gross manager fees: management fee plus performance fee. This percentage reduces as your AUM grows.

Setup costs
Pay only legal & registration fees (approx $7k, passed through at cost by POD). After that, you face no further fixed fees, and are charged only via POD's fee sharing structure.
Base fee structure

Our base fee is a percentage of your fund's total gross manager fees, applied progressively across AUM tiers. The more AUM you manage, the lower our blended take rate.

Tiers are applied progressively, like income tax brackets. A $30m fund pays 25% on the first $10m of fees, 20% on the next $15m, and 15% on the final $5m, resulting in a blended rate between 15% and 20%.

Capital introduction fee
POD Discover: built-in allocator network
POD operates a growing network of allocators who can browse and invest in funds directly through the platform. If capital comes to your fund via POD's Discover platform, an additional fee applies on the fees generated by that capital only.

Cap intro fee: 20% of gross fees attributable to capital introduced via POD Discover. This is charged on top of the base take rate, and only on the portion of your AUM that came through the network. Capital you raise independently is not subject to this fee.
Third-party costs
What you pay to third parties
  • POD is a licensed Fund Administrator, so no additional third-party fund admin is required. We do not charge any minimum fee for admin services, simply a flat 0.20% on AUM, which you pass through to investors as an expense line item. With all other savings, we expect your investor expense ratio to be well below 1%.
  • Any minimum fees charged by third-party providers (e.g. custodians, banks, auditors) are absorbed by POD at the umbrella level. You never face a third-party minimum fee.
  • Variable costs we do pass through (custody, banking, audit) are charged at wholesale rates negotiated at the umbrella level, significantly cheaper than fees charged to an individual fund.
  • Trading volumes are also merged at the umbrella level, resulting in lower trading fees for managers on the POD platform.
